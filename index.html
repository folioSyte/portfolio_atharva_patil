My Works

Recent works

  • URDF Robotic Arm

    Used ROS and RVIZ for building a Simple Robotic Arm, learned about Its working, rigid body motion, degrees of freedom, robot links & joints, and understood the usage of ROS in real-world industrial applications.

  • Nissan Leaf HVAC System

    Modeled numerical model of Nissan’s Leaf HVAC system. The model carried out on the Simulation Tool Scilab Xcos is based on the recently implemented HVAC system of the Car.HVAC’s purpose in a vehicle is to clean, cool, heat, regulate, ventilate, and dehumidify the air entering the cabin, depending on the operator’s inputs and electronic sensors.

  • KTM Duke 390 ABS Unit

    Modeled and simulated ABS numerical model of KTM DUKE 390 using Scilab and Xcos. I analyzed the factors such as stopping distance without ABS, stopping distance with ABS, stopping distance at various vehicle speeds, the Influence of vehicle mass on stopping distance, Studying brake pressure at different conditions, and achieving the slip coefficient.

  • Technical Report on Battery Management System

    Worked on overview of common concepts for BMS hardware, starting with a collection of generic requirements, for which, later on, implementation considerations were taken as an aspect for the development of the sustainable environment by reducing the use of fossil fuel-consuming cars.
